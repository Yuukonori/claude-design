Transient notification; tone shows as a left accent rule (the one place a thin colored rule is allowed).

```jsx
<Toast tone="success" title="Code generated" message="Copied to clipboard." onClose={dismiss} />
<Toast tone="danger" title="Export failed" message="Check your connection." />
```

Tones: `neutral` | `success` | `warning` | `danger` | `info`. Stack bottom-right in app chrome.
